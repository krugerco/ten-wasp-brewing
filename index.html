The Story Behind the Sting

In spring 2023, Jacob and Diana drove through Sumner, Iowa — and immediately knew this was home. Within weeks, they'd committed to transforming a stunning 1898 Main Street building into something the town had never seen: its very first brewery.

Nine months of hands-on renovation followed. They exposed original brick walls, restored the handsome tin ceiling, and repurposed antique bowling alley lanes into the bar counters where you now set your pint. Every detail tells a story.

Jacob brings a professional brewing pedigree spanning Mankato Brewery, Urban Growler, Voyageur, and Rhombus Guys. He rarely repeats a recipe — with 78+ beers produced and counting. Two of his creations were named Iowa's Best Beers of 2025.

Giving Back

The Community Hive

Every pint you pour here does more than refresh — it reinvests in Sumner. Through monthly Brewery Tours and register round-up donations, Ten Wasp is committed to giving $5,000+ per year to local organizations through the Sumner Community Causes program.

2024 Annual Giving Goal

One featured local cause per month. Every tour ticket counts.

Ten Wasp is also a proud member of the Iowa Brewers Guild and part of the Iowa Beer Passport program — putting Sumner on the craft beer map statewide.
